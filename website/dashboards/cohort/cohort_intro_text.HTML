Cohorts allow us to track outcomes for a group of defendants whose cases were opened in the same year. These cohorts are further narrowed to focus on defendants identified as belonging to a shared racial/ethnic group. A general template for interpreting the statistics on this page is detailed below:

1. Of [race] individuals arrested and prosecuted on a [charge category] in [year], [#] had [outcome].

a. Eg. Of Black defendants arrested and prosecuted on a felony in 2019, 3,337 had bail requested at criminal court arraignment.

2. [%] of [race] individuals arrested and prosecuted on a [charge category] in [year] had [outcome].

a. Eg. 71.9% of Black defendants arrested and prosecuted on a felony in 2019 had bail requested at criminal court arraignment.
